Quartz is our most common mineral. Quartz is made of the two most abudant chemical elements on Earth, Oxygen and Silicon. Atoms of Oxygen and Silicon join together as Tetrahedrons (three sided pyramids). These stack together to build crystals. Billions of Tetrahedrons are needed to build even a small crystal.
